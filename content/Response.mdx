This section focuses on how best to respond to the COVID-19 outbreak, seeking to answer questions like is a vaccine available and what supplies are recommended.

In the wake of the outbreak, there are thousands of recommendations being made, enough that it becomes difficult for ordinary citizens to sort through them to determine what it means to them, and how they might best prepare and respond. This section will organize the answers according to basic questions in order to make them easier to tackle.
