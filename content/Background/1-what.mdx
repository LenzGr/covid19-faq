---
title: 'What is COVID-19?'
description: 'This page describes what COVID-19 is.'
---

COVID-19 is a disease caused by [SARS-CoV-2](https://www.mayoclinic.org/diseases-conditions/coronavirus/symptoms-causes/syc-20479963), a virus from the coronavirus family. Others in this family are responsible for maladies such as the common cold. Its symptoms include fever, cough and respiratory complications. Symptom severity ranges from mild to severe, and can result in death.
